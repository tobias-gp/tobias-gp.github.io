I am an AI Architect at Porsche with a focus on Generative AI and Machine-Learning Infrastructure. I contribute to academic research in the field of Ubiquitous Computing and Human-Computer Interaction. In particular, I uncover new ways of perceiving and interacting with the environment by making sense of data. Previously, I was a researcher in the Sensors & Devices Group at Microsoft Research Cambridge and a Morgan Fellow at Downing College . I obtained my PhD from TU Darmstadt, working at Fraunhofer Institute for Computer Graphics Research IGD .
